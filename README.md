# 📊 Sales & Product Performance Dashboard  
## Freshway International Trading (FMCG), Qatar — Power BI Project

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **sales performance for February and March** using **Power BI**, focusing on salesperson performance, product contributions, and sales variance analysis.

The dashboard helps monitor sales targets, evaluate performance trends, and understand the relationship between **salespersons and the products they sell**.

---

## 🏢 About the Company

**Freshway International Trading (FMCG), Qatar** is engaged in the trading and distribution of fast-moving consumer goods.

The company deals with:

- 🥬 Vegetables  
- 🍎 Fruits  
- 🫒 Edible Oil  
- 🌴 Dates & Nuts  

Freshway Trading aims to provide seamless buying and selling experiences while maintaining operational excellence in global trading.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze sales performance by comparing **actual sales vs target sales**
- Evaluate **monthly sales variance and performance trends**
- Compare **individual salesperson performance**
- Analyze **product-wise sales contribution**
- Identify **top and least performing salespersons**
- Understand correlation between **salespersons and products sold**

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales  
- Total Target  
- Target Achievement (%)  
- Monthly Sales Variance  
- Sales Variance (%)  
- Month-over-Month Growth (%)  
- Average Sales by Salesman  
- Total Quantity Sold  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 🏠 Page 1 — Sales Overview & Salesman Performance

Includes:

- KPI Cards (Sales & Target Performance)
- Monthly Sales Comparison
- Salesman Performance Analysis
- Variance Analysis Table
- Interactive slicers for filtering

**Purpose:**  
Monitor overall performance and compare salesperson results.

---

### 📦 Page 2 — Product Performance Analysis

Includes:

- Product-wise Sales Contribution
- Quantity Sold Analysis
- Salesperson vs Product correlation
- Top & Least performing product insights
- Interactive filters

**Purpose:**  
Understand product demand and salesperson-product relationships.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights Generated

- Performance comparison across salespersons
- Product contribution analysis
- Monthly growth and variance tracking
- Identification of high and low performers
- Quantity-based product performance evaluation
